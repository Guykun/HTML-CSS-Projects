Unlock Your Potential as a Pianist

"Key!Board" is a teaching program for musicians or asipiring musicians to learn or brush-up on their skills on the keyboard!

  • Learn at YOUR pace

    Have fun with your experience! Let the pace you learn at be natural! In a literal sense we can slow down or speed up the songs you choose!



  • Read Sheet Music

    Learning to read sheet music will allow you to learn to play any piece you desire! Our system detects the notes you play in response to your song's sheet music! You have the ability to play all of your favorite songs!



  • Practice Reminders

    Practice, Practice, Practice! We help keep you on track with reminders during your chosen practice days. Remember, practice makes perfect!

Tribute to Robin WIlliams

Robin Williams, was a well known, beloved actor and voice actor to people of all ages. It was rare to not see a smile on his face.

Here is a list of some of his famous roles in movies:

  • Sean Maguire - Good WIll Hunting (1997)
  • Alan Parrish - Jumanji (1995)
  • John Keating - Dead Poets Society (1989)
  • Genie - Aladdin (1992)
  • Peter Pan - Hook (1991)
  • Daniel Hillard - Mrs. Doubtfire (1993)
  • Adrian Cronauer - Good Morning Vietnam (1987)
  • Patch Adams - Patch Adams (1998)
